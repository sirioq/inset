Case Study 2

Teaching
Quantum Physics

Bringing the hardest A-Level topics to life with interactive simulations

The Problem

Quantum Physics is counterintuitive by nature.

The problem

Topics like the photoelectric effect and emission spectra are impossible to observe directly in a school lab, and static diagrams don't convey the physics.

What was needed

Interactive simulations where students could adjust variables and observe the consequences — paired with structured lesson plans and worksheets.

The Process
